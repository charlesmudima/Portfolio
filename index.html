GIT713 Overview

GIT713 provides an introduction to professionalism, ethics, legislation, strategy and implementation in the geographic information system industry. These were taught principles are taught through the implementation of a project and various presentations in class. Lastly, the module also provides an understanding to data management and quality. From my journey and experience thus far in GIT713, I have seen that the module aims to provide a rich and practical understanding regarding professional practice in the Geographic Information Systems (GIS) industry. This module covered a range of topics, which I will reflect on and discuss. The topics covered include; GIS Strategy and Implementation; Professionalism and ethics in GIS; Business communication; Project and Software development; GIS data management; and a Botanical Garden group project. I acknowledged that it is important to note that GIT713 and the GIS industry is comprised of various components.
